Goals:

Frusterations:

User Story

"As a patron I want to know about latest offerings and about ABV."

Use Case

Pre-conditions: she's logged in and has an idea of what she's looking for

Post-conditions: she's found her preferred beer, and a brewery

Frequency of use: once or twice a week

Scenario: It's Sunday afternoon, and she wants to find a nearby brewery with a kitchen that carries a fruity IPA over 6% ABV

Interaction Flow

U. From user dashboard, Deva enters preferences into beer style finder looking for IPA

B. Browser removes breweries that don’t serve IPA

U. Deva selects descriptors for fruity beer

B. Browser removes breweries that don’t carry “fruity” IPA

U. Deva selects ABV for over 6 percent

B. Browser removes breweries that don’t carry ABV over 6 percent

U. Deva selects kitchen filter

B. Browser returns results

U. Clicks nearest locator pin

B. Opens beer information dialogue

U. Deva loves the beer, clicks upvote
