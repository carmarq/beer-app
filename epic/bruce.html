User Story

“As a brewer I want to update my profile with my latest beers.”

Use Case

  • Pre-conditions: On homepage (already logged in)
  • Post-conditions His profile is updated with his latest offerings
  • Frequency: Once or twice a month
  • Scenario: It's a thursday night and Bruce just kegged a new batch of strawberry rhubarb gose and
    to inform patrons of this new limited offering before the weekend.
  • U:Bruce navigates to "add beer".
  • B:Browser displays "add beer" form.
  • U:Bruce enters date into all fields.
  • B:Browser enters date into all fields and clicks submit.
  • U:Bruce clicks confirm.
  • B:Browser returns dialog choise to either go home or add another beer.
  • U:Bruce clicks "go home".
  • B:Browser returns Bruce to his dashboard with update project info.
